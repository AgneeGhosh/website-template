This project brings the top 20 highest-rated IMDb movies to life with a dynamic bar chart built in D3.js. As each bar animates into view, you can instantly see how these iconic films stack up based on their IMDb ratings. It’s a fun, visual way to explore and compare some of the most-loved movies of all time!

This D3 chord diagram gives you an easy, visual way to explore what’s in your favorite foods! Each section shows a nutrient, and the connecting ribbons reveal how much of it is in each menu item—perfect for seeing which foods pack in the protein or sneak in the extra fat. It’s a handy tool for anyone curious about making more informed, balanced choices on their plate.
